Our Story

Sign Tech was established in February, 2007 and has been operating for 13 years until now. We are very grateful for all the customers who have supported us along this 13 years and also very welcome to all the potential customers that will be experiencing our service. Sign Tech mainly specializes in Graphic Design, Colour Separation, Acrylic Laser Cutting, Vinyl/ Sticker/ Backlit Printing, Offset Printing, PP Board (Silk Screen) Printing, LED/ 3D/ SignBoard/ Billboard/ Car Sticker Installation and other services.

What We Do

With a vision to innovate, we create the corporate object that you want, by offering expert advice and quality solutions in designing, printing and installation services for your jobs. We have the digital printing systems to meet speed, print quality and cost-effectiveness that give you added flexibility, and on-time completion of your projects. Moreover, we do also have an installation team for applying Acrylic Boards, LED Boards, 3D Signages and Billboard at your desired place.
